Campus Safe Space

This project is an anonymous campus confession and support platform where students can share their thoughts freely and safely. Users can log in using Google, post confessions, and interact with the campus community through likes and reactions.

The main goal of this project is to provide a safe digital space for students to express feelings, experiences, and concerns without revealing their identity.

Technologies used: React.js, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, Google OAuth 2.0
